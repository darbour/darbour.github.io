About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate, working in the Knowledge Discovery Laboratory at the College of Information and Computer Sciences of UMass Amherst. My advisor is David Jensen. My research interests lie in causal learning in relational data, nonparametric dependence testing and statistical relational learning.
